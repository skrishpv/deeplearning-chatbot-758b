To Launch the chat bot follow the below steps.

1. Go to Command prompt/terminal and enter the project folder.
2. Once within the project folder enter command 'python ./chat.py'
This will launch the chat bot in the terminal.

You can say hello, hi etc. You can aske her name or what she is?. You can ask for help. You can ask steps to create an account. you can ask for how to log a complaint. Say thanks and goodbye.

Currenltly the goodbye messages are not being classified accurately due to training error. But this is a version one and I am working on Version 2 with another approach.
